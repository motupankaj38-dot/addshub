FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Is the ad budget included in the package?
No, the ad budget is not included. Our pricing covers the service fee — creative design, campaign setup, and management. The Meta ad spend (what Facebook/Instagram charges to run your ads) is paid separately and directly to Meta. We always explain the total cost clearly before starting.
How soon can my ads start running?
After we receive your business details and the creative is approved, most campaigns go live within 24–48 hours.
Do you create reels and ad creatives?
Yes! All our ad plans include creative design — images, reels, and ad copy. You don't need to provide any creative assets or hire a separate designer.
Do you manage both Facebook Ads and Instagram Ads?
Yes. We manage both Facebook and Instagram ad campaigns. You can run them separately or together depending on your goals and budget.
Can I upgrade my package later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade from a daily to weekly or monthly plan at any time. Just reach out to us on WhatsApp and we'll help you make the switch smoothly.
How long does website development take?
Our standard business website is delivered within 7 business days after we receive your content and requirements. We keep the process fast and efficient so you can go live quickly.
Do you provide support after project completion?
Yes. We provide post-delivery support via WhatsApp. For ongoing campaigns, we're available throughout the campaign duration. For websites, we offer basic support after handover.
How do I get started with AddsHub?
Simply fill out the consultation form on this page or message us directly on WhatsApp at +91 96618 38049. We'll understand your goals, recommend the right plan, and get your campaign running as quickly as possible.
What is included in the ad plans?
All our ad plans include creative design (images/reels), campaign setup, audience targeting, and ongoing monitoring. You don't need to hire a separate designer or ad manager.
Are the reach numbers guaranteed?
No. The reach and click numbers shown in our pricing are estimates based on typical campaign performance. Actual results vary based on audience competition, industry, ad quality, and Meta's algorithm. We are transparent about this and will never promise specific results.
Do I need a Facebook Business page to run ads?
Yes, a Facebook Business Page and/or Instagram Business Account is required to run Meta Ads. If you don't have one, we can guide you through the setup process at no extra charge.
Can I cancel or pause my campaign?
Yes. Campaigns can be paused or stopped at any time. For daily plans, you simply don't renew. For weekly and monthly plans, we'll discuss the process when you sign up. We believe in flexibility and transparency.
